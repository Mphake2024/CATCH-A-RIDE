About Catch-a-ride

Access to safe, reliable, and affordable transportation remains a persistent challenge in Lesotho, especially in urban centers like Maseru and remote rural areas. Public transport options are often inconsistent, informal, and lack digital integration, leaving passengers with limited choices and unpredictable travel experiences.

Catch-a-ride is designed to transform how people move around Lesotho. We offer a dependable, transparent, and user-friendly ride-hailing service that not only addresses transportation inefficiencies but also creates new economic opportunities for drivers and small fleet owners.

Our commitment is to the Basotho communities, providing a service that prioritizes safety, convenience, and affordability, while also contributing to a more sustainable urban environment.

How It Works

Getting started with Catch-a-ride is simple and straightforward.

1

For Riders: Request a Ride

Download the Catch-a-ride app (or use our SMS service for basic booking). Enter your destination and request a ride or parcel delivery instantly.

2

For Riders: Track & Travel

Track your driver's arrival in real-time. Enjoy a safe, comfortable, and efficient journey to your destination or watch your parcel reach its recipient.

3

For Riders: Pay & Rate

Pay securely through the app. After your trip, rate your driver and provide feedback to help us maintain high-quality service.

1

For Drivers: Sign Up & Go Online

Register as a Catch-a-ride driver. Once approved, simply go online in the app to start receiving ride or parcel delivery requests.

2

For Drivers: Accept & Navigate

Accept nearby requests. Our smart routing guides you to your passenger/parcel and then efficiently to their destination.

3

For Drivers: Earn & Grow

Complete trips and deliveries, earning income with every successful journey. Grow your business with a reliable platform.

Our Impact

Catch-a-ride is more than just a transportation app; it's a catalyst for positive change in Lesotho. By promoting shared mobility, we actively contribute to reducing traffic congestion and lowering greenhouse gas emissions, fostering cleaner urban environments.

This not only enhances user satisfaction but also empowers local individuals by creating vital economic opportunities for drivers and small fleet owners, strengthening the local economy.

Our vision is to build a sustainable and thriving mobility ecosystem that serves the unique needs of Basotho, ensuring everyone has access to safe, reliable, and respectful transportation.
